    Why do I get "Account has been disabled" when logging into my Account?

    This is actually a safety-mechanism in case someone attempts to get to your account through bruteforcing.

    This doesn't immediately mean that someone is trying to hack into your account, but it's probably you forgetting your own password or mistyping it multiple times.

    If this happens, the one who tries to login (probably you) get locked out from logging in on any account. Creating new accounts is basically pointless if you get locked out. Other people will still be able to login on their account just fine if they don't have this very issue.

    If you have waited 1 hour, you should best immediately refresh your login as you can accidentally disable your account again by checking the leaderboards, levels, etc.
